About The Build

I’ve owned this 2007 Toyota FJ Cruiser since new. I’ve had a few different stereo set-ups in this thing over the years. Most recently I decided I wanted an SQ build this time around. SQ refers to sound quality, so not just loud but clear and nuanced. All the work was done by myself.

  • Head Unit - Pioneer NEX Receiver
  • Midbass in the doors - Audison AP8 Prima 8”
  • Midrange in dashboard - Hertz MP 70.3 3”
  • Tweeter in the pillars - Hertz MP 28.3
  • Subwoofers - 2 10” Arc Audio Black Series
  • Custom built fiberglass subwoofer enclosure
  • Front Stage Amplifier - Digital Designs SS4a
  • Subwoofer Amplifier - Digital Designs M1d
  • Digital Sound Processor - Arc Audio PS8
  • Chromecast Audio with a mobile router installed
  • Hi-Resolution Bluetooth Receiver
  • Sound deadened entire interior with Second Skin products
  • Second deep cycle battery kit - Dirty Parts
